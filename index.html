What is Veltrix TV? +

Veltrix TV is a premium IPTV streaming service that gives you access to 24,000+ live TV channels, 160,000+ movies and TV series on demand — all in stunning 4K quality. Think of it as replacing your cable subscription with something better and cheaper.

What devices can I use? +

Veltrix TV works on Smart TVs (Samsung, LG, Sony), Android phones & tablets, iPhones & iPads, Amazon Fire Stick, Windows & Mac computers, MAG boxes, and Enigma receivers. Basically any device with a screen.

How fast do I get my login? +

Within 5-15 minutes after payment, your login credentials will be sent directly to your WhatsApp. During peak hours it may take up to 30 minutes, but we always deliver as fast as possible.

Can I watch on multiple devices? +

Each subscription is for one device connection at a time. If you need multiple simultaneous connections, contact us via WhatsApp and we'll set you up with a multi-device plan at a discounted rate.

Is there a free trial? +

Yes! We offer a free trial so you can test the service before committing. Simply message us on WhatsApp and we'll set you up with trial access to see the quality for yourself.

Do you offer refunds? +

Absolutely. We stand behind our service with a satisfaction guarantee. If you're not happy with Veltrix TV, contact us within 7 days and we'll process a full refund. No questions asked.

What if I have buffering issues? +

Our anti-freeze servers are optimized for smooth playback, but if you experience any issues, our 24/7 support team will troubleshoot with you immediately. Most issues are resolved within minutes by adjusting your player settings or connection.
